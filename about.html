What Verx Is

An infrastructure holdings company

Verx is the parent structure behind a group of companies. It doesn't compete in any single market — instead, it provides the infrastructure each of its subsidiaries is built on: ownership, governance, capital, and the legal and operational foundation that lets a business stand and scale.


Where a subsidiary goes from there is up to the subsidiary. Each one chooses its own sector and charts its own course. CredMe, the first, chose fintech. Verx stays the constant beneath them all.
