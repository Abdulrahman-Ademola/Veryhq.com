Subsidiary 01 · Fintech
CredMe
Know your credit. Get the loan.

CredMe is a fintech platform that helps people in Nigeria understand and build their credit — and get matched with lenders likely to approve them.

It securely brings together someone's financial activity across their bank accounts, turns it into a clear credit profile, and shows them exactly where they stand and how to improve. When they're ready to borrow, CredMe connects them to the right lenders — and gives those lenders pre-qualified, loan-ready applicants in return.
